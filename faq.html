What does a number on a team mean?

The number in front of the tip suggests the confidence in the tip. The scale is from 1 to 10. Higher number stands for higher confidence in the tip, but also lower rate most of the time. The number is followed by the tip on the match winner. Thus,for example "9 on Chelsea" means that Chelsea will most likely win the match.
